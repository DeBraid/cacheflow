Projects I've been involved with.

Devocracy.org

Fall 2013

Devocracy helps students find and fund scholarships. It gives anyone the freedom to donate to a student or cause, and student the opportnity to sell themselves and their dreams.

Still in development, Devocracy.org was a project born out of Bitmaker Labs, a 12-week intensive course on software developement. Built in collaboration with Eric Stiens and Omar Khafagy.

Create interactive graphics with d3.js

Here is the final iteration of the interactive map shown in the video. Also live is a statistics page with dynamic data-driven charts which render beautifully on your phone or tablet.
